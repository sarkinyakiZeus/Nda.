Welcome

The Nigerian Defence Academy (NDA) was established on 5 February 1964 to train officers for the Armed Forces of Nigeria. Before then, it was known as the Royal Military Forces Training College (RMFTC). After independence in 1960, it became the Nigerian Military Training College.
